3x+ay=10
5x+by=20
by-(5/3)ay=20-(5/3)10
  =(b-(5/3)a)y

y = (20-(5/3)10) / (b-(5/3)a)
  = 10/(3b-5a)
  = -16.66667 +/- Dy

We use a single order Taylor approximation for small nonstatistical errors in a and b

Dy = |dy/da|*|Da|+|dy/db|*|Db|
|dy/da| = 10/(3b-5a)^2 * 5
|dy/db| = 10/(3b-5a)^2 * 3

at the particular values of a and b,
(dy/da*Da,dy/db*Db) = (6944.444444444412, 4166.666666666647)
Dy = 0.1
=> y = -16.7 +/- 0.1
